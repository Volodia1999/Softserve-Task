History of the team

FC Barcelona is a Catalan football club from the city of the same name, one of the best in Spain and in the world. Founded in 1899 by a group of Swiss, British, Spanish and Catalan footballers headed by Joan Gamper. The club has become one of the symbols of Catalonia. Representatives of Barcelona, ​​together with representatives of Real Madrid and Athletics from Bilbao, were at the origin of the examples. In this case, “Barcelona” is among the three teams that did not leave the highest Spanish division. The other two are all the same "Real" and "Athletic." The first serious successes came to the “Barcelona” in the late 50s, when the club headed Elenio Herrerer. Then the Catalan team managed to win the European Cup for the first time - the Fairs Cup. It was then that the first great Barça began to be created.
